# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) – Brand & Homepage Project

## Project Overview

This project presents a simple brand identity and responsive homepage for the **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)**. The goal of this project is to design a clean, modern logo and build a single-page website that communicates the institute’s mission, services, and community.

The design reflects the institute’s focus on **digital risk, cybersecurity, and AI governance**, combining academic insight with real-world industry practice.

---

# 1. Logo Design

## Concept

The logo is based on a **geometric cube structure**. The cube represents:

- **Structure** – organised digital risk frameworks
- **Protection** – layered cybersecurity and governance
- **Resilience** – the ability to manage and respond to digital threats

The layered cube also symbolizes the institute’s model of **training, innovation, and deployment**, where professionals build knowledge step by step to manage complex digital systems.

## Color Palette

The logo uses a simple and professional colour scheme:

- **Orange** – Innovation, energy, and technological advancement
- **Black** – Security, authority, and strength in risk management
- **White** – Clarity, transparency, and trust in governance

This colour combination creates a strong contrast and ensures visibility across digital platforms.

## Logo Variants

Two versions of the logo were created:

1. **Icon-only logo**
   - Cube symbol
   - Used for favicon, mobile header, and small spaces

2. **Full logo**
   - Cube icon + text "Institute of Digital Risk"
   - Used for website headers and branding

Both versions are designed to remain clear and readable at small sizes.

# 2. Homepage Design

The website is built as a **single-page responsive website** using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## Key Sections

### Hero Section

Introduces the institute with a strong headline and call-to-action.

Example elements:

- Headline: _Advancing the Future of Digital Risk_
- Subheading explaining the mission
- CTA button (Explore Programs / Hire Talent)

### About IDR

Explains the institute’s purpose as an **industry-led training and deployment institute** that prepares professionals for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated environments.

The section highlights the connection between **academic collaboration and industry practice**.

### Service Pillars

Three core areas of the institute are presented as cards:

1. **Academy**
   Training programs and bootcamps for students and professionals.

2. **Innovation & Incubation**
   Development of digital risk frameworks, AI governance models, and intellectual property.

3. **Advisory Services**
   Support for organisations implementing standards such as NIST and ISO 27001.

### Community

Describes the audience served by the institute:

- Students
- Early-career professionals
- Risk practitioners
- Financial services sector
- Critical infrastructure professionals

### Contact / Call to Action

A simple form allows visitors to register interest or contact the institute.

Fields included:

- Name
- Email
- Message
