About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and technology risk roles within regulated, high‑consequence environments — from critical infrastructure to financial services.

We bridge academic insight from leading UK university partners with real‑world project delivery. Our fellows work on live industry challenges while developing next‑generation risk frameworks, AI governance models, and operational resilience.

At a glance

  • ✓ Academic partners: 3 Russell Group universities
  • ✓ 120+ practitioners deployed since 2022
  • ✓ Industry members: AON, National Grid, Lloyds

Our model: train · innovate · deploy

🎓 Academy

Intensive bootcamps and certifications for students & professionals. Curriculum co-designed with industry to address NIST, ISO 27001, NIS2, and AI governance.

⚡ Innovation & Incubation

We develop intellectual property and future risk models. Our incubator supports startups focused on digital risk, AI safety, and cyber resilience.

📋 Advisory Services

Hands‑on help for organizations to implement frameworks like NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2. Our deployable experts work inside your team.

Institute of Digital Risk

Design rationale: The cube icon symbolises structure, stability, and the multi‑dimensional nature of digital risk — suggesting resilience and a systematic approach. Orange conveys energy, caution, and innovation, while black and white ensure contrast and a minimalist, professional aesthetic. The clean sans‑serif (Inter) gives a modern tech/education feel, legible at all sizes. The geometric forms reflect the precision required in high‑consequence environments.
