About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry‑led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and technology risk roles within regulated, high‑consequence environments — from critical infrastructure to financial services.

We bridge academic insight from leading UK university partners with real‑world project delivery. Our fellows work on live industry challenges while developing next‑generation risk frameworks, AI governance models, and operational resilience.

At a glance

  • ✓ Academic partners: 3 Russell Group universities
  • ✓ 120+ practitioners deployed since 2022
  • ✓ Industry members: AON, National Grid, Lloyds
